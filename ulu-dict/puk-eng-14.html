2. Financial. Palapala ʻaiʻē, pila hōʻaiʻē; noka, noka kālā, nota dala.

3. Musical. Leo mele, hua, hua mele. Rare: kānāwai hīmeni, kānāwai mele. Grace note, hua liʻiliʻi, hua kōkua. Leading note, hua alakaʻi. Lowest or highest note (do), pā. Whole note, hua ʻokoʻa, poʻo. Half note, poʻolima, hua hapa. Quarter note, hua hapahā, poʻoʻele. Second note (re), kō. Third note (mi), lī. Fourth note (fa), hā. Sixth note, lā. Seventh note (ti), mī. Eighth note, poʻomana. Sixteenth note, poʻomanalua. Thirty-second note, poʻomanakolu.
