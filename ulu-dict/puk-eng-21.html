Maʻamau, mea mau. Also: paʻa mau, kūmau, kuluma, hana mau ʻia. Same as usual, ʻoia mau nō.

usurer, usurious

Puni kuala.

usurp

Kāʻili hewa, lawe ʻāpiki, kāʻihi.

usury

Uku hoʻopaneʻe, kuala.

Utah

Uka, Uta; Mauna Pōhaku (poetic).

utensil

Ipu, mea paʻahana.

utmost

Poʻokela, nui loa.

utter

Hoʻopuka, puaʻi, See say.

utterance

ʻŌlelo, māpuna leo, māpuna ʻōlelo, puana.
