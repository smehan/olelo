ʻĀ, ʻaʻā, hōʻā, puhi, kuni, puhi ahi; — intensely, hāʻena, hūʻaʻā, puoko; — weakly, ʻomī, ʻomīmī, mīmī; —, as with anger, ʻā. Also: pāwela, kā liliko, liko, likoliko, kūʻena, makawela, pāpaʻa wela, hoʻowela, liholiho, nōʻā. See back, smart, and saying, ʻaʻā 1. To burn cane, puhi kō. Scar from burning, kākala wela, paʻawela. To burn maliciously, puhi manaʻo ʻino. The throat burns, ʻaʻā ka puʻu (great thirst).

burned

Pau ahi, wela, welawela, pāpaʻa, kunia; — completely, puhi pau; —, as a kukui nut candle, kōkolikoli. Also: pāwela, pāpaʻapū, pāpaʻa wela. See sunburned.
