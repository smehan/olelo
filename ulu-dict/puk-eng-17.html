Q

q

No Hawaiian term.

quack

Kakā.

quadrangle

Huinahā, huinahā like ʻole, huinahā lua like.

quadrilateral

Huinahā.

quadrille dance

Hoʻowilimoʻo.

quadruplets

Hānau pāhā.

quagga

Kuaka.

quail

Manu kapalulu. Rare: kakakō, kēlū, pauʻaka.

quaint

ʻAno ʻē. See old-fashioned.

quake

Haʻalulu, naue, naka, kualakai, kūhulukū. See earthquake. The earth quakes, nei ke ʻōlaʻi.

Quaker

Kueka.

qualifications

Nā mea e kūpono ai; nā hoʻokūpono ʻana.

qualified

Mākaukau, kūpono.

quality

ʻAno, kūlana. Poor quality, ʻano maikaʻi ʻole; kakakē (rare). Moral qualities, pono.

qualmish

Pāmaʻi, poluea.

quantity

Nui. Also: kuaniki, puʻu. Great quantities, laupaʻi, ʻaʻole i laua aku, ʻaʻole o kana mai, ā hewa. Small quantity, ʻaʻohe nui, lihi, iki.

quarantine

Hoʻomalu maʻi. Quarantine station, hale hoʻomalu. Animal quarantine station, wahi hoʻomalu holoholona.

quarrel

Hoʻopaʻapaʻa, paio, pāonioni, pāoni, kūʻēʻē, pāʻumeʻume, hukihuki, hakakā, hākā ʻōlelo. Also: ʻāpuni, paʻapaʻa, kāmoko.

quarrelsome

Nanā, ihu nanā; kole ka waha (see kole); nahunahuihu, kāpēkeʻu, wawaʻu.

quarry

Lua ʻeli pōhaku, lua kā koʻi (adze).

quart

Kuaka.

quarter

Hapahā; koaka (rare). Quarter to five, hapahā i koe, kani ka hola ʻelima.

quarterly

Hapahā. Quarterly report, hōʻike hapahā.

quartermaster

Kūhoe.

quarter note

Poʻoʻele.

quartet

Pūʻulu hīmeni pāhā, leokū pāhā.

quavering

Haʻalulu, haʻihaʻi.

quay

Uwapo, uapo.

Quebec

Kuipeka, Kuibeka.

queen

Mōʻī wahine, aliʻi wahine, kuini; wahine (in playing cards).

queen bee

Nalo meli mōʻī wahine.

queer

ʻAno ʻē. See homosexual for slang usage.

quench

Kinai.

quenched

Kena, kinaiʻia.

query

Ui, nīnau.

question

Nīnau, ui. To question repeatedly, ninaninau, nīele, neno. To ask a leading question, nīnau pākākā. To question indirectly, hoʻohuahualau, hoʻonīnau.

question mark

Kiko nīnau.

questionnaire

Palapala ninaninau, pepa hoʻopihapiha, paʻi hakahaka.

quick

ʻĀwīwī, koke, māmā, wiki, wikiwiki, wawe, hikiwawe, ʻeleu, ʻemo ʻole, alawiki. Also: ala-, miki, ʻāwiki, ʻākepa, ʻakeu, hue, kaʻukaʻu lua ʻole, kāholo, alapine, alapinepine, elemimo, alamimo, lauahi, hoʻokanahē, kōʻala, kōʻalaʻala, ālulu, ulele, akeake, ʻakakē, ʻeʻea, puahia, muku, panehū, panaleʻa, naʻenaʻe. To learn quickly, ʻaʻapo. To make off with quickly, mio, miomio, alamio.

quicksand

One pohō, one mimiki.

quicksilver

Waikālā, heu.

quick step

Hele wiki.

quick-tempered

Huhū wale, pōkole ka naʻau, naʻau pōkole, lelepī, nonohuwā, pīkananā, pīkanalā, ioioleʻa. See hot.

quick time

Hele wiki.

quiet

1. Quality. Mālie, hoʻomālie, hilu, wailana, malu, maluhia, hoʻomalu, hoʻolaʻi, laʻi, pohu, malino. Also: kuapapa, nihi, kaulana, hālana, ōpūhea, kuʻinehe, kuakapu, laʻikū, palanehe, kōnale, kāpehe, kūpalaiki, haumalu, maialile, noʻenoʻe, mimo, miha, hāmiha, mahū, pū. To move quietly, hele mālie, nihi ka hele, kaʻa niau. To do quietly, hana mālie, kakakihi. Wierdly quiet, ano, kuʻinehe. To think quietly, lana mālie ka manaʻo. To keep quiet, noho mālie. Quiet sea, kai malino, mālie ke kai, kai kū. Be quiet! Kulikuli!

2. Transitive. Hoʻonā, hoʻonānā, hoʻomalimali, hoʻomaʻalili.

quieted

Akakuʻu, nā. See quiet.

quill

Hulu, hulu kākau (for writing).

quills

Wanawana.

quilt

Kapa kuiki; kapa ʻāpana, kapa lau (with appliquéd designs); kapa pulu (padded); kapa pohopoho, kapa poho (patchwork, crazy); kīhei pili, kapa pili (lined). Base of quilt on which pattern is appliquéd, kahua, honua. Poles used in quilt making, kua. To quilt, kuiki. Quilt patterns or designs, lau, kuiki. For names of designs see kuiki. Other designs: hae-Hawaiʻi, hala kahiki, kua-honu, kuli-puʻu, lala, makani-kāʻili-aloha, niho-wili-hemo, pika-waiohinu, ʻulu-hua-i-ka-hāpapa (see ʻulu). To follow designs in quilting, kuiki lau. To quilt in a direction away from the quilter, hoʻopiʻipiʻi. To finish a space in a quilt, hoʻopaepae.

quintet

Pūʻulu hīmeni pālima, kuinikeke.

quintuplet

Hānau pālima.

quit

Haʻalele, waiho. Quit it! Uoki! To quit work, haʻalele i ka hana; ʻolohani.

quitclaim

Palapala hoʻolilo.

quite

Nō, wale. Quite good, maikaʻi nō, maikaʻi wale.

quiver

1. Shake. Kapalili, haʻalulu, naka, kualakai, līʻō, lule, lulelule, hālulelule. Also: haʻukeke, ʻapi, pilu, ʻolokā.

2. Arrow case, ʻAʻa pua.

quiz

Hōʻike pōkole, nīnau hōʻike, nīele, hoʻonīele, hoʻohuahualau.

quoits

Kilu, ʻume.

quorum

Hapa nui, kolamu.

quota

Mahele (share); ka mea i makemake ʻia, ka heluna i makemakeʻia (desired quantity).

quotation

Ka mea iʻōlelo ʻia, ʻōlelo.

quotation marks

Kauna koma.

quote

ʻŌlelo, wahi.

quotient

Helu puka.
